Sad Eyes

Beneath the light heaven's sprawling gaze,

Lies Madeleine, whose eyes light the day.

Like pastries golden on the edge and sweet,

Her spirit strong, not one to easily beat.

In her, a paradox you'll meet,

A tender heart, hardened with heat.

Her essence, warm, a soft retreat,

Yet strong as steel, and just as fleet.

Her gaze holds stories of a thousand cries,

Yet in her sorrow, a captivating guise.

With every glance, she weaves a spell so deep,

A promise that her heart is hers to keep.

Madeleine, a paradox, serene and wild,

In her, the beauty of both storm and child.
